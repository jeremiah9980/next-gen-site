Bala Product Notes

GameChanger owns the game. NextGen owns the team brand.

Answers for Bala on target customers, business model, data strategy, and multi-tenant team sites for NextGen Team Site, Rally OS, and RallyIQ.

Read the AnswersTeam Ops Story
Core Idea

Do not replace GameChanger.

In select softball, GameChanger is already the default scoring, streaming, video, recap, and stats platform. NextGen should sit around it as the branded public home for the team.

Answers for Bala

Four questions. One clear product lane.

01 · Target Customers

Who are the target customers?

The buyer is the team, coach, club, or parent-run organization. The users are parents, family members, players, sponsors, and fans.

02 · Business Model

What is the business model?

Start as team-site SaaS. Charge monthly or annually for a branded site with roster, schedule, player profiles, sponsors, media, fundraising, and GameChanger links.

03 · Game Data

How are we getting the data?

Use manual entry, CSV/stat imports, and deep links first. Explore deeper integrations later where the platform rules allow it.

04 · Multi-Tenant Sites

Does each customer get their own site?

Yes. Every team gets its own branded site and domain experience, while the backend stays multi-tenant.

GameChanger is the field. NextGen is the front door, player showcase, sponsor layer, and story engine.

Product Roadmap

Build the branded layer first. Add intelligence second.

1

NextGen Team Site MVP

Roster, schedule, player profiles, sponsors, photos, announcements, fundraising, and links to GameChanger.

2

Team Admin OS

Simple dashboard for coaches or parent admins to manage content once and publish it everywhere.

3

Game Data Layer

Import stats, link games, attach media, and enrich player pages without trying to beat GameChanger at scoring or streaming.

4

RallyIQ

AI-generated recaps, player summaries, weekly team stories, recruiting cards, highlight prompts, and sponsor reports.

5

Rally OS

Expand from one team to clubs, tournaments, multi-team organizations, fan engagement, sponsor inventory, and content automation.

Pitch Note

NextGen is the branded team layer.

GameChanger is where the game gets scored and streamed. NextGen is the branded team site and RallyIQ is the intelligence layer that turns existing activity into player visibility, sponsor value, and automated team storytelling.
