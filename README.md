# Next Gen Team Site

A prospect-facing landing page and pricing pitch for youth sports team websites.

## What this sells

Next Gen Team Site gives select youth sports teams a clean digital home for:

- Home page with team branding and social media profiles
- Roster card page with player photos, jersey numbers, and profile links
- Individual player pages with GameChanger-ready stat snapshots
- Player character profiles, coach notes, and development focus
- Film room / video clip wall
- GameChanger schedule and stats layout
- NCS tournament dashboard / tracker
- Tournament scoreboard with pool, standings, bracket, and next-game views
- Fundraising and sponsor visibility
